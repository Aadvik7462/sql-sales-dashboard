📊 1. Project Overview
This project analyzes Superstore sales data using SQL to find trends in sales, profit, customers, and product performance.
The goal is to extract business insights for decision making.

📈 2. Sales Analysis
- Total sales were calculated using SUM(Sales)
- Sales trend shows variation across months
- Some months perform significantly better than others

💰 3. Profit Analysis
- Profit varies across regions and categories
- Some segments generate high revenue but low profit
- Profitability is not uniform across the business
👥 4. Customer Insights
- A small group of customers contribute major sales
- Top customers can be targeted for loyalty programs
📦 5. Product Insights
- Certain products dominate total sales
- Some categories perform better than others
- Product performance is uneven across catalog
🌍 6. Region Analysis
- Some regions are significantly more profitable
- Regional performance varies widely
- Focus should be on high-performing regions
🎯 7. Final Business Conclusion

This is VERY important:

- Focus on high-profit categories and regions
- Improve underperforming segments
- Strengthen customer retention strategies
- Optimize product mix based on performance
